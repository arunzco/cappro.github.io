The Ultimate Tool for Investment-Minded Agents

CapPro is the must-have app for real estate agents focused on multifamily investments. Making it easier than ever to present and analyze multifamily investments and their cash flow, giving agents and investors the tools they need for clearer, more efficient deal-making.

Specifically designed to streamline the underwriting process, CapPro helps you effortlessly calculate essential investment metrics like Net Cash Flow, Cap Rate, DSCR, NOI, and more, all in one place. Whether you’re working with buyers or preparing to present a deal, CapPro’s intuitive platform generates fully customizable, brandable reports that showcase property returns in a professional, easy-to-understand format.

With CapPro, you can input property details, add notes, images, and documents. You can instantly generate a comprehensive property information sheet with all the details investors need to know. This sheet includes income and expense data, along with all the visuals and notes you need to present a deal effectively. The underwriting feature allows you to quickly calculate investment metrics along with the monthly cashflow for the property. Save your database of buyers and sellers and create easily shareable reports, empowering investment agents to put together more deals than ever before.

Key Features of CapPro:

  • Branded Cash Flow Reports: Effortlessly calculate and generate professional, branded cash flow reports, providing clients and investors with clear, actionable insights.
  • Organize Property Information: Stay on top of all your potential deals by uploading and storing detailed property information, including images and key data, in one easily accessible location.
  • Effortless Sharing: Seamlessly share your generated reports, property details, and images with your database
  • Customizable Agent Profiles: Tailor your professional profile to suit your brand, creating a personalized experience for clients and investors alike.
  • Advanced Investment Metrics: Calculate essential property returns and metrics, empowering your clients to make smarter and quicker investment decisions.
  • Multi-Phased Reports: Generate and store multi-phase reports (e.g., current cash flow, pro-forma cash flow) to provide a comprehensive financial outlook for each property.
  • On-the-Go Property Information: Easily gather all the necessary property details directly from the app, whether you're in the office or on the move, ensuring you're always prepared for your next deal.

Key Investment Metrics:

  • Net Cash Flow
  • Cash on Cash Return
  • Capitalization Rate (Cap Rate)
  • Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR)
