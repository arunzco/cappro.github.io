About our app

The CAPPRO app empowers users to conduct thorough buy-and-hold property analyses effortlessly through a user-friendly mobile interface. By consolidating crucial property details such as expenses, ownership costs, and income sources, the app performs essential investment performance calculations, aiding in decision-making processes.

Designed for real estate professionals, the cappro mobile app facilitates efficient property data management with an intuitive interface. Its key features encompass centralized data collection, automated calculations, document creation, and effortless sharing, streamlining underwriting analysis. Simplifying property investment assessments, this app condenses robust analytics and management tools into an accessible mobile solution.
